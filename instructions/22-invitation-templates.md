# Invitation and reminder templates

Ready-to-edit messages for the campaign in [21-communications-plan.md](21-communications-plan.md). Replace everything in [brackets]. Keep them short; people skim.

Placeholders used below: 

- `[ORGANIZATION]`
- `[SENDER NAME]`
- `[SENDER ROLE]`
- `[SURVEY LINK]`
- `[CLOSE DATE]`
- `[CONTACT EMAIL]`

---

## Warm-up (optional, a few days before launch)

**Subject:** A short open source survey is coming

> Hi everyone,
>
> Next week we'll send a short, anonymous survey about how [ORGANIZATION] uses and supports open source software. It takes about 12 minutes, and your answers will shape the support we build. Keep an eye out for it.
>
> Thanks,
> [SENDER NAME]

---

## Invitation (launch day)

**Subject:** Help shape open source support at [ORGANIZATION] (about 12 minutes)

> Hi [first name / everyone],
>
> We're trying to understand how [ORGANIZATION] develops, manages, and supports the open source software it relies on, and where people would value more support. Your input will directly inform what we offer next.
>
> **Take the survey:** [SURVEY LINK]
>
> It takes about 12 minutes and is anonymous. You don't need to be an expert. For many questions, "not sure" is a perfectly good answer. Please respond by **[CLOSE DATE]**.
>
> Questions? Just reply, or email [CONTACT EMAIL].
>
> Thanks for taking part,
> [SENDER NAME], [SENDER ROLE]

---

## Endorsement add-on (optional, from a leader)

Use as a short line at the top of the invitation, or as a separate note.

> A note from [LEADER NAME], [LEADER ROLE]: "Open source matters to our research and teaching. Please take a few minutes for this survey. It will help us decide where to invest."

>

>

>
> Thanks,
> [SENDER NAME]

> Hi everyone,

>
> If you'd like that summary sent to you directly, just email [CONTACT EMAIL].
>

> [SENDER NAME]
