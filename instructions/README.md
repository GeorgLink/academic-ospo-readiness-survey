# How to run the survey

These guides take you from the template file to a finished analysis, in four steps. They were written for LimeSurvey 6.17.9; other versions may look a little different. The file numbers are XN, where X is the step and N is supporting material for that step.

## Slides (optional)

Prefer a visual walkthrough? The training deck covers the whole process step by step, with screenshots and speaker notes:

- [Academic-OSPO-Readiness-Survey-Training.pptx](Academic-OSPO-Readiness-Survey-Training.pptx): editable PowerPoint (speaker notes included)
- [Academic-OSPO-Readiness-Survey-Training.pdf](Academic-OSPO-Readiness-Survey-Training.pdf): PDF

## Step 1: Set up

- [10-setup.md](10-setup.md): create or open your LimeSurvey, replace the placeholders, import the template, make the few edits your institution needs, configure the settings, and activate.
- [11-data-handling-statement.md](11-data-handling-statement.md): a ready-to-adopt privacy statement for the introduction.

## Step 2: Distribute

- [20-distribute.md](20-distribute.md): decide who to reach, send the survey out, lift the response rate, and close it.
- [21-communications-plan.md](21-communications-plan.md): timing, channels, and reminder cadence.
- [22-invitation-templates.md](22-invitation-templates.md): ready-to-edit invitation and reminder messages.

## Step 3: Analyse

- [30-analyze.md](30-analyze.md): export the responses and produce the results (the spreadsheet, by hand, or the optional notebook).

## Step 4: Interpret

- [40-interpret.md](40-interpret.md): what the numbers mean and how to turn them into priorities.
- [41-using-ai-to-analyze.md](41-using-ai-to-analyze.md): optional guidance on using an AI assistant with the results, with privacy notes.

You will need your own LimeSurvey instance, self-hosted or hosted by LimeSurvey. This package does not include hosting, so your responses stay under your control. The full question list is in [`../survey/survey-questions.md`](../survey/survey-questions.md), the importable template in [`../survey/academic-ospo-readiness-survey-template.lss`](../survey/academic-ospo-readiness-survey-template.lss), and the analysis tools in [`../analysis/`](../analysis/).
