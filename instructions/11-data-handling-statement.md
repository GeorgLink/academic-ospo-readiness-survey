# Data-handling statement

This is a ready-to-adopt statement you can place in the survey's introduction (it replaces `[DATA-HOSTING STATEMENT]`). It is written to be widely applicable, including for institutions that fall under the GDPR.

> **Please have this reviewed.** This is a template, not legal advice. Before you launch, have your institution's data-protection officer, privacy office, or equivalent review and adjust it. Fill in every placeholder in square brackets. This note covers **data protection**; for **research-ethics / IRB** approval, see the "Before you launch" note in [10-setup.md](10-setup.md).

> Your responses are collected and stored on [ORGANIZATION]'s own LimeSurvey instance, hosted [WHERE, e.g. "on servers managed by our central IT" or "by LimeSurvey GmbH in the EU"]. Responses are anonymous: we do not collect your name or IP address. We use the results only to understand and improve open source support at [ORGANIZATION]. Aggregated, anonymised results may be shared more widely. Data is handled in line with [APPLICABLE LAW OR POLICY, e.g. the GDPR and [ORGANIZATION]'s data-protection policy]. We keep the raw responses until [RETENTION, e.g. "the project is complete, and no longer than 24 months"], after which they are deleted. For questions or to exercise your data rights, contact [CONTACT NAME] at [CONTACT EMAIL] [and/or the data-protection office at [DPO CONTACT]].

## Longer version (for a linked privacy notice, if you want one)

**Who is collecting this data.** [ORGANIZATION] ([DATA CONTROLLER / OFFICE]) is running this survey to assess and improve its open source practices and support.

**What we collect.** Your answers to the survey questions.

**What we do not collect.** We do not record your name or IP address with your answers. The survey is configured for anonymous responses.

**Where it is stored.** On [ORGANIZATION]'s own LimeSurvey instance, hosted [WHERE]. Access is limited to [WHO, e.g. "the project team"].

**Why we process it (legal basis).** [LEGAL BASIS, e.g. "our legitimate interest in understanding and improving open source support," or "your consent," as confirmed by your data-protection office].

**How long we keep it.** Raw responses are kept until [RETENTION] and then deleted. Aggregated, anonymised results may be kept and shared indefinitely.

**Your rights.** You can withdraw at any time before submitting. Depending on the law that applies to you, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because responses are anonymous, we may be unable to identify your individual response after submission. To exercise your rights or ask questions, contact [CONTACT NAME] at [CONTACT EMAIL] [or the data-protection office at [DPO CONTACT]].

**Sharing.** We share only aggregated, anonymised results. We do not sell your data or share individual responses.
